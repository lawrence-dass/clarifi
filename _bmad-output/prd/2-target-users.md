# 2. Target Users

**Primary:** Canadian individuals aged 25-45 who actively manage their finances and use multiple bank accounts or credit cards.

**Secondary:** Fintech interviewers at RBC, TD, Wealthsimple, Koho, and similar Canadian companies evaluating this as a portfolio project. The one-click **Public Demo Access** (§11) exists specifically to serve this persona — zero-friction evaluation without signup or a real bank.
